About Me

Learn more about my educational background, professional experience, technical skills, projects, and future goals in software development.

Hello! I'm Avijit Kumar Lenka, a passionate Computer Lab Programmer, Technical Trainer, and Software Developer with a strong commitment to continuous learning and innovation. My academic journey began at Ravenshaw Collegiate School where I completed my 10th standard under the Board of Secondary Education, Odisha in 2012. I then pursued Higher Secondary Education (+2 Science with Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics) from Christ College in 2014.

In 2018, I completed my Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Electrical Engineering from CV Raman Global University. Although my academic background is in Electrical Engineering, my passion for technology led me toward software development, web technologies, and programming.

Over the years, I have developed expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Core Java, Advanced Java, MySQL, Spring Framework, Spring Boot, Microservices Architecture, REST APIs, GitHub, Postman, and Software Testing Tools. I have successfully completed 7+ projects that demonstrate my ability to design, develop, and deploy modern software applications.

As a Technical Trainer, I enjoy sharing knowledge and helping students build strong technical foundations. Along with software development, I am proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint for documentation, reporting, and presentations.

Currently, I am expanding my skills by learning Python, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), PHP, C Programming, and the MERN Stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js). My goal is to become a versatile full-stack developer capable of building intelligent and scalable applications for the future.
